Slippi Ranked Stats

A desktop app for tracking and analyzing your Slippi Ranked sets. Point it at your replay folder and get a full breakdown — matchups, stages, sessions, rating history, and per-set grading against real community data.

What it does

Replay scanning

Scans your local Slippi folder and pulls in all your ranked sets automatically.

Matchup & stage stats

Win rates against every character and stage, sortable by best and worst.

Session breakdown

Each play session tracked separately with a momentum chart.

Rating history

Track your rating over time with a set-by-set breakdown.

Set grading

Every completed set scored across Neutral, Punish, and Defense against real community baselines.

Auto-updates

Notified in-app when a new version is available — installs in one click.

Premium

Live session tracking (real-time per-game stats, rating delta) and the full per-stat set grade breakdown are available to supporters on Ko-fi or Patreon. The overall grade and core stats are free for everyone.
