A desktop app for analyzing your Slippi Ranked sets.

Point it at your replay folder and get a full breakdown of your play — matchups, stages, sessions, rating history — plus a grade for every set across Neutral, Punish, and Defense, measured against real ranked data. Runs locally; nothing is uploaded.

What it tracks

Everything Slippi already knows about your sets

Replay scanning

Point it at your local Slippi folder and it pulls in every ranked set automatically.

Matchup & stage stats

Win rates against every character and stage, sortable by best and worst.

Session breakdown

Each play session tracked on its own, with a momentum chart for the sets in it.

Neutral

Opening rate, first-hit conversion, and how often you win the first exchange.

Punish

Damage per opening, edgeguard conversion, and kills relative to real ranked data.

Defense

Recovery rate, stock duration, and survival — graded against your exact matchup.

Premium

An always-on stream overlay, plus the full grade breakdown

Your rank, Rating, and season record on screen the whole set — then your grade and Rating change the moment it ends. No editing, no manual updates: point OBS at a local file and it just runs. Also unlocks the full per-stat grade breakdown with matchup-specific baselines. The overall grade and core stats stay free for everyone.

Runs entirely on your machine — no gameplay data ever leaves it.
